Who we are?

Hello! I am Shristi Kumari, a 3rd year B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering student.
This project is an e-commerce platform created for a hypothetical brand named "Miss Lehenga Designer", which specializes in stunning lehengas for various occasions. Through this project, I aim to expand my knowledge of full stack development and improve my technical skills, particularly in frontend technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
My primary goal is to create a user-friendly and visually appealing interface that enhances the shopping experience. Additionally, this project allows me to explore essential concepts such as responsive design, interactive elements, and performance optimization. It is a perfect blend of creativity and technical learning, fueling my passion for crafting elegant digital solutions. This initiative is not just an academic exercise but also a stepping stone toward my aspiration of excelling in the field of full stack development.

This project is made from HTML, CSS, JS.

Explore our exclusive collection of lehengas designed for weddings, parties, and special events. From traditional to contemporary styles, we offer a variety of lehengas, including Wedding Lehengas, Partywear, Occasional, Cape, Anarkalis, Mermaid styles, and more.
